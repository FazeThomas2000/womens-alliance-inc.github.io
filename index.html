Empowering Young Women Through Education, Leadership, and Opportunity

For more than 60 years, Women’s Alliance, Inc. has supported the academic success and personal growth of young women in Greater Cincinnati through scholarships, mentorship, and community engagement.

WHO WE ARE

Women’s Alliance, Inc. is a Cincinnati-based nonprofit organization dedicated to expanding educational and financial opportunities for young women. Through scholarship awards, mentorship, and leadership development, we help students build confidence, independence, and pathways to long-term success.

OUR IMPACT

For decades, Women’s Alliance has turned generosity into meaningful outcomes.

  • 60+ years of service in Greater Cincinnati
  • Scholarships awarded annually to deserving students
  • Community-driven fundraising that directly supports education
