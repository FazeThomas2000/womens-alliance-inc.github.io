Help Us Send Deserving Students to College

Women's Alliance, Inc. is a 501 (c)3 nonprofit organization and your donation is tax deductible. The mission of Women's Alliance, Inc. , is to support the education, cultural and social development of youth in Greater Cincinnati and Dayton. Each year, we contribute a minimum of $10,000 in scholarships to graduating high school seniors attending an accredited college or university. These scholarships are provided to assist with tuition, textbooks, and or to provide a stipend for fees. Each is renewable for up to four years of study. We are able to do this because of the many generous donors who have supported our efforts over the years. Partner with us to help ensure that our children receive the education they so richly deserve.
