What Is Hats Galore?

Hats Galore is Women’s Alliance’s annual fundraising brunch where community members come together in celebration and sisterhood. This joyful event features great food, festive hats, and meaningful moments—all in support of young women pursuing higher education.

All proceeds directly fund Women’s Alliance scholarships and programs

Event Highlights

  • Brunch and mimosas
  • Best Hat contest
  • 2026 Scholarship recipient presentation
  • Silent auction
